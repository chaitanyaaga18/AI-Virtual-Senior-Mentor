7. Associate Software Engineer

Company: Accenture

Job Title

Associate Software Engineer

India (Multiple Locations)

Accenture is hiring Associate Software Engineers to develop and maintain enterprise applications while working with cross-functional teams to deliver technology-driven business solutions.

Knowledge of Java, Python, or C++.

Preferred Qualifications

Familiarity with cloud platforms and DevOps tools.

Strong problem-solving and communication skills.
