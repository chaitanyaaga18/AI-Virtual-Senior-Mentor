14. Data Analyst

Company: Capgemini

Job Title

Data Analyst

Location

India (Multiple Locations)

Job Summary

Capgemini is seeking Data Analysts to analyze business datasets and provide insights that support data-driven decision making.

Key Responsibilities

Collect and analyze large datasets.

Identify trends and patterns in data.

Create reports and dashboards.

Collaborate with business stakeholders.

Basic Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or Data Science.

Knowledge of SQL and Excel.

Basic understanding of data analysis techniques.

Preferred Qualifications

Experience with Power BI or Tableau.

Knowledge of Python or R.
