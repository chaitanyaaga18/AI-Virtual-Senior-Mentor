1. Software Development Engineer Intern

Company: Amazon

Job Summary

Amazon is seeking talented students for the Software Development Engineer (SDE) Internship program. Interns work alongside experienced engineers to design and develop scalable systems that serve millions of customers worldwide.

Key Responsibilities

Collaborate with engineers to design and build innovative products and services.

Design and develop scalable solutions in large distributed computing environments.

Build distributed storage, indexing, and query systems.

Participate in code reviews and contribute to technical documentation.

Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code following best practices.

Work in an agile development environment.

Basic Qualifications

Pursuing Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.

Knowledge of programming languages such as Java, Python, or C++.

Understanding of data structures and algorithms.

Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Preferred Qualifications

Experience with distributed systems.

Knowledge of AWS cloud services.

Previous internship or software projects.
