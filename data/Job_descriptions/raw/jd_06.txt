6. Data Analyst Intern

Company: Deloitte

Job Title

Data Analyst Intern

Location

Bangalore / Gurgaon, India

Job Summary

Deloitte is seeking a Data Analyst Intern to support business teams in analyzing large datasets and generating actionable insights. The intern will assist in building analytical models, creating dashboards, and supporting decision-making processes through data-driven analysis.

Key Responsibilities

Analyze structured and unstructured datasets to identify trends and insights.

Create data visualizations and dashboards using BI tools.

Perform data cleaning, transformation, and validation.

Assist in developing analytical reports for stakeholders.

Support business teams in data-driven decision making.

Basic Qualifications

Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Data Science, or related field.

Knowledge of SQL, Python, or R.

Familiarity with Excel and data visualization tools.

Preferred Qualifications

Experience with Power BI or Tableau.

Understanding of statistical analysis and data modeling.
